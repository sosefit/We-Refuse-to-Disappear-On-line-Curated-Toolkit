How to Use This Toolkit

This curated resource toolkit is designed for flexible, targeted use by educators, administrators, activists, and family members seeking to create affirming learning spaces for trans and non-binary students. Whether you are designing curriculum, planning professional development, organizing community action, or advocating for policy change, you will find resources here to support your work.

Each section is organized into curated categories to support critical engagement with the scholarship, policy landscape, and emerging activism around trans education:

  • Recommended Readings for foundational and advanced scholarship
  • News Articles & Substacks for timely analysis and emerging discourse
  • Policy Reports and Policy Trackers to ground your work in current legal and legislative contexts
  • Political & Social Organizations and Activist Groups & Campaigns to connect with advocacy and collective action
  • Podcasts & Webinars for accessible, ongoing learning
  • Open-Access Curricula & OER Repositories for ready-to-use and adaptable teaching materials
  • Films, Documentaries & Visual Media to support critical engagement and discussion

You may choose to "Show all categories" to explore the full range of resources, or select specific categories that align with your interests and needs.
